About Me

Ever since I was a child, I had always wanted to go into a career that involved making things, so for the longest time I said that I wanted to be an engineer, without really knowing what that meant. It wasn't until I reached highschool that I decided that I wanted to combine that desire with my love of video games, which lead to me pursuing a career in video game programming.

I am interested by almost every aspect of video game design, from the game theory to the actual programming fundamentals. I find myself most facinated with the intricacies of creating a good UI , and so I tend to consider myself to be a bit of a UI snob.
